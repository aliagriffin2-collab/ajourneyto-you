Learn About Communities

Explore foundational cultural groups commonly included in Ethnic Studies. Each subsection includes a brief definition sourced from reputable references.

African American Studies

Focuses on the history, culture, and experiences of people of African descent in the United States, including the impacts of enslavement, resistance, and ongoing cultural contributions (Encyclopedia Britannica).

Asian American Studies

Examines the experiences of people with origins in East Asia, Southeast Asia, and South Asia living in the United States, including immigration histories, identity, and community activism (Asian American Studies Association).

Native American / Indigenous Studies

Centers the histories, sovereignty, cultures, and lived experiences of Indigenous peoples of the Americas, emphasizing their diverse nations and long-standing cultural traditions (Smithsonian NMAI).

Latina/o/x Studies

Explores the experiences of people with cultural or national origins in Latin America, including migration stories, language diversity, and cultural heritage (Pew Research Center).

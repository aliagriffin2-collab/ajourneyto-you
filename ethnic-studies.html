Understanding Ethnic Studies

Ethnic refers to large groups of people classified by shared cultural, linguistic, national, religious, or ancestral origins (Merriam‑Webster). Ethnic Studies is an academic field that examines the social, historical, cultural, and political experiences of racial and ethnic groups—particularly marginalized communities.

For children, Ethnic Studies helps build early understandings of identity, fairness, belonging, and global diversity.
